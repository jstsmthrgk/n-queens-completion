SAT to NQC

This is not yet finished, but already a lot of fun.

SAT

Satisfiability of a CNF

3-SAT

Satisfiability of a CNF with a maximum of three variables per clause

3,3-SAT

Satisfiability of a CNF with a maximum of three variables per clause and a maximum of three occurences per variable

SNQCRCD

Set of NQCRCD Problems where D+ is the empty set, which interact on the Sumdiagonals.

NQCRCD

N-Queens Completion with sets for allowed Columns and Rows and forbidden Difference- and Sumdiagonals. There are no queens given.
